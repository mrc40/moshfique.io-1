ABOUT ME



A career spanning 9 years, I began with making short films and that is when I fell in love with direction. Having now directed over 60 audiovisual, TVC and OVCs. Also I’m equipped with a 3D Animation and VFX diploma, paving the path for the modern styles for advertising and filmmaking. I’ve worked as Chief AD for over 150 television single fictions, popularly known as ‘Natok’, over 20 serials. And has been the editor of more than 350 video projects. Working within very tight deadlines is my specialty. And he delivers every-time.
